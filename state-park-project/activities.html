ACTIVITIES

Plan Your Adventure!

Arkansas State Park offers over 50,000 activities annually, including guided hikes, lake tours, birding adventures, kayak tours, living history demonstrations, nature games, historic site tours, bald eagle watches, spring wildflower walks, campfires, outdoor skills workshops, and A.D.E.-accredited teacher professional development. These activities aim to provide a meaningful park experience and make memorable memories.
