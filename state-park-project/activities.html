ACTIVITIES

TOURS AND EVENTS

The visitor center exhibits and self-guided tours are free. Any guided activities require a fee.

Guided tram tours are offered on Sundays, Wednesdays, and Saturdays at 2 p.m. These tours are subject to change so call in advance to confirm availability. Tram tours are also available by advance request. Group rates are available.

KNAPP TRAIL

Plum Bayou Mounds Archeological State Park

Trail Type: Barrier Free (ADA), Day Hikes | Difficulty: Easy

Time: 0.8 Miles

Hiking times may vary.

What to know: This fully accessible trail and boardwalk leads visitors through this prehistoric Indian site. See three of the 18 mounds which once stood within the sites earthen embankment. A guided tour discusses the Prehistoric mound building Native Americans who inhabited the area over a thousand years ago.

THE PLUM BAYOU TRAIL

Plum Bayou Mounds Archeological State Park

Trail Type: Day Hikes | Difficulty: Easy

Time: 1.6 Miles

Hiking times may vary.

What to know: Grassy trail that leads visitors through the entire pre-historic Native American site. A self-guided tour will enable visitors to get a glimpse into the past when the Plum Bayou Indians inhabited this area over 1,000 years ago.
