About

The 153 Outfitters is a premier destination for adventure on the America's First National River, The Buffalo River in Northwest Arkansas. The Bear Ridge cabin retreat offers a cozy haven amidst nature's splendor, perfect for all ages. The company offers top-of-the-line rental canoes, Buffalo Canoes, designed for both beginners and seasoned adventurers. The 153 Outfitters is more than just a rental service; it's a gateway to the great outdoors. Our great brand embodies the spirit of adventure, encapsulated in our logo, slogan, and website. With vibrant colors, sleek design, and a touch of wilderness charm, the 153 Outfitters captures the essence of what makes the Buffalo River truly special. Join us and let us guide you on an adventure of a lifetime, whether exploring Bear Ridge or paddling along the majestic Buffalo National River.

Bruce (Left)
Older than the gravel in the Buffalo River, Bruce Bauer retired from the world of Tech and Information Technology to return to his first love: the great outdoors. He and his business partner Thomas Wallace now operate an outdoors experience company near Jasper Arkansas in the heart of the Buffalo River valley. Bruce enjoys sharing his outdoor knowledge with visitors and encourages sustainability teaching the Leave No Trace principles developed by LNT.org

Thomas (Right)
Thomas Wallace, age 48, recently retired from a distinguished career in Environmental Science to pursue a lifelong passion for outdoor adventures. After spending over two decades in academia, Thomas decided to embrace the natural beauty of the Ozarks full-time. Now residing in Newton County, Arkansas, he has opened a canoe outfitter on the scenic Buffalo River. The outfitter provides guided tours and equipment, focusing on sustainable outdoor practices and environmental education. Thomas’s deep knowledge of the local ecosystems and commitment to conservation make the outfitter a unique spot for both seasoned paddlers and novices eager to explore the natural wonders of Arkansas.
